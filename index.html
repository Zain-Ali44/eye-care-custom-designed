Our Services

Offering 3 Kind of Services.

Eye Examination

Why choose to have an eye examination at Enfield Eye Care? The simple answer is that we care about the health of your eyes. Almost all of our patients return to us year after year and most of our new patients come to us through word of mouth.

Eye Condition Service

What sets our services apart from the rest of the industry is our attention to detail and receptiveness to the unique needs of each patient/client. Our clinicians are all highly qualified, professional and experienced and will carry out a thorough examination.

Brand/Glasses brand

we value the importance of good service trust and quality and almost all of our patients return year after year. All of our spectacles are assessed by the strictest quality control measures for accuracy and glazing to ensure that you are receiving the best made pair of spectacles possible.
